IBPS PO ExamPattern– Prelims, Mains & Interview!

The IBPS is an important exam that aspirants want to clear for entry into the Banking sector. Every year lakhs of candidates register for this competitive exam. If you are among those candidates appearing for IBPS PO then you must know the exam pattern before starting to prepare for the exam. Candidates have to clear each exam with a sectional cutoff to secure a place in the final merit list.

  • SSC Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL)
  • SSC Combined Higher Secondary Level (SSC CHSL)
  • SSC Stenographer C & D (SSC Stenographer)
  • SSC Multitasking Staff (SSC MTS)
  • SSC Junior Engineer (SSC JE)
  • SSC Junior Hindi Translator (SSC JHT)
  • SSC General Duty Constable (SSC GD)
  • SSC Central Police Organization & SI (SSC CPO)
  • SSC Selection Post
Tip: It is really important to focus on the grammar section when answering the descriptive questions.IBPS PO Interview

Candidates who have qualified for the mains exams will be called for a personal interview. The date of the interview will be announced after the IBPS PO mains exam is over. The personal interview will be off 100 marks. Candidates have to score at least 40% marks to get on the merit list. After the interview round is over, IBPS will display the merit list on the official website.
